CTO as a Service is a **defined engagement** with clear scope, cadence, and outcomes: you get senior leadership without hiring a full executive bench before you are ready.

## Problems solved

- **Architecture drift**: decisions accumulate until every change is expensive. We establish a north-star architecture and a practical roadmap to get there.
- **Delivery risk**: releases are infrequent and stressful. We introduce governance that increases throughput without lowering standards.
- **Vendor and integration complexity**: contracts, SLAs, and technical alignment across suppliers—managed as engineering work.
- **Team scaling**: hiring plans, skill gaps, and engineering practices—grounded in what your product actually needs next.

## How it works

1. **Baseline** (weeks 1–2): systems map, risks, constraints, and stakeholder alignment.
2. **Operating model** (ongoing): weekly leadership cadence, architecture reviews, and decision logs.
3. **Delivery integration**: work with your teams and partners; we do not sit outside the critical path.
4. **Metrics**: define a small set of measurable indicators—quality, throughput, incidents, and technical debt trends.

## Outcomes

- Clear technical direction tied to business milestones
- Fewer surprises in production and vendor deliveries
- A team that learns patterns—not one-off heroics
